How Bagdrop Works

Bagdrop is a revolutionary platform that redistributes transaction fees back to token holders through airdrops.

Here's how it works:

  • Transaction Fees: Every transaction on the Bagdrop network generates a small fee
  • Fee Collection: These fees are collected into a community pool
  • Regular Airdrops: Fees are automatically distributed to all BAG token holders proportionally
  • Real-time Updates: Watch live transactions and holder rankings update in real-time

The more BAG tokens you hold, the larger your share of the airdrop fees. It's a sustainable way to reward long-term holders and active community members.
